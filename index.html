Who Are We?

We are Grant High School's robotics team in Portland, OR. We participate in local robotics competitions like BunnyBots and FRC. Grant Robotics has been a creative and resourceful team since 2007. We have won 1st and 2nd place in BunnyBots several times, and we look forward to doing it again with the help of our amazing sponsors and mentors!
